ABOUT US

De’Pemblin Tour & Travel was established on 09 December 2016 with the vision of offering sincere, reliable & memorable travel experiences in the hills of North Bengal

We specialise in customised tours, thoughtfully designed to suit the unique preferences of every traveler. 
For guests who already have their plans in place, we also offer well structured itineraries to ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey

At present, our services focus on day tours across Darjeeling, Kalimpong, Kurseong and Mirik, allowing visitors to experience the charm, culture and natural beauty of these remarkable hill destinations

Our fleet includes comfortable luxury vehicles as well as hatchbacks, maintained to ensure safety and convenience throughout your journey. 

Our tour managers are highly experienced with the hilly terrain and possess extensive knowledge of the regions points of interest, ensuring that your travel experience is both safe and enriching

In addition to out travel services, we proudly run and operate ASRAYA HOMESTAY in Darjeeling, a warm and welcoming 04 room homestay where the guest can enjoy a peaceful and authentic stay in the hills

At De’Pemblin we believe in serving our guests with honesty, integrity and genuine hospitality
