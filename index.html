About Us

De'Pemblin Tour & Travel was established on December 9, 2016 with the vision of offering sincere, reliable & memorable travel experiences in the hills of North Bengal. We specialise in customised tours, thoughtfully designed to suit the unique preferences of every traveler.

Based in Darjeeling, we serve guests with honesty, integrity and genuine hospitality. Our experienced tour managers possess extensive knowledge of the region's points of interest, ensuring your travel experience is both safe and enriching.
