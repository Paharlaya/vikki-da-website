DPTT04

Darjeeling Hills Charm

3 Nights / 4 Days

Overview

An extended Darjeeling experience with a full day excursion to Mirik. This package offers a perfect balance of sightseeing and leisure time, allowing you to soak in the mountain atmosphere at a relaxed pace.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling (3 nights with Mirik excursion) → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling

  • Meet & greet at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Transfer to Darjeeling
  • Check-in to hotel on arrival
  • Evening free for acclimatization
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Full Day Sightseeing

Early Morning (4:00 AM):

  • Tiger Hill sunrise viewing
  • Ghoom Monastery
  • Batasia Loop

After Breakfast (9:30 AM):

  • Ropeway experience
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute
  • Padmaja Naidu Zoo
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Centre
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden
  • Japanese Temple

Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Mirik Excursion

  • After breakfast, drive for Mirik excursion
  • Stop at Indo-Nepal border for shopping
  • Visit Mirik Lake (Sumendu Lake)
  • Explore cardamom plantations
  • Visit tea estates
  • Optional boating at the lake
  • Return to Darjeeling by evening
  • Evening free for Mall Road walk
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 4: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out from hotel
  • Transfer to NJP/IXB Airport
  • Tour ends

Inclusions

  • 3 nights accommodation on twin sharing
  • Daily breakfast
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Mirik excursion
  • All applicable taxes
