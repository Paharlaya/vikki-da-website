DPTT03

Lagoon Splendour 02

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Experience Darjeeling first, then enjoy the scenic beauty of Mirik on your return journey. This reverse route allows you to end your trip with the tranquil charm of Mirik's lake and tea gardens before heading to the airport or station.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling (2 nights) → NJP/IXB Airport via Mirik

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling

  • Meet & greet at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Direct transfer to Darjeeling
  • Drive through scenic mountain roads
  • Check-in to hotel on arrival
  • Evening free for shopping or leisure
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing

Early Morning (4:00 AM) - 3 Points:

  • Tiger Hill - Sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • Ghoom Monastery visit
  • Batasia Loop

After Breakfast (9:30 AM) - 8 Points:

  • Ropeway
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed)
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed)
  • Museum (Thursday closed)
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed)
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden (Sunday closed)
  • Japanese Temple

Evening free. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Departure via Mirik

  • After breakfast, check out from hotel
  • Transfer to airport/station via Mirik
  • Stop at Indo-Nepal border for shopping
  • Visit Mirik - explore:
  • Man-made Sumendu Lake
  • Cardamom plantations
  • Tea estates
  • Boating opportunity (direct payment)
  • Continue to NJP/IXB Airport
  • Tour ends

Inclusions

  • Accommodation on twin sharing basis
  • Food as per meal plan
  • Vehicle for transfers & sightseeing
  • All permit fees & hotel taxes
  • Mirik excursion on departure day
