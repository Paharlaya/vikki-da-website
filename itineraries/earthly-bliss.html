DPTT05

Earthly Bliss

4 Nights / 5 Days

Overview

Experience the ultimate hill station tour covering three beautiful destinations. From Darjeeling's colonial charm to Mirik's serene lake and Kalimpong's flower markets - this comprehensive tour offers the best of North Bengal hills.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling (4 nights) + Mirik + Kalimpong → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

  • Airport/Station pickup
  • Transfer to Darjeeling
  • Hotel check-in
  • Evening at leisure

Day 2: Darjeeling Sightseeing

  • Tiger Hill sunrise
  • Ghoom Monastery & Batasia Loop
  • Full day local sightseeing
  • Tea garden visit

Day 3: Mirik Excursion

  • Full day Mirik excursion
  • Sumendu Lake visit
  • Tea estates & cardamom plantations
  • Indo-Nepal border shopping
  • Return to Darjeeling

Day 4: Kalimpong Excursion

  • Drive to Kalimpong via Lamahatta
  • Durpin Hill viewpoint
  • Deolo Hill
  • Flower nurseries
  • Science Centre
  • Return to Darjeeling

Day 5: Departure

  • Check out after breakfast
  • Transfer to airport/station
  • Tour ends
