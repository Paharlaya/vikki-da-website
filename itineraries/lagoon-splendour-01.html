DPTT02

Lagoon Splendour 01

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Begin your journey with the scenic beauty of Mirik before reaching Darjeeling. This tour combines the tranquil charm of Mirik's Sumendu Lake with the grandeur of Darjeeling's mountains. Experience tea estates, cardamom plantations, and the Indo-Nepal border shopping experience.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling via Mirik (2 nights) → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival via Mirik

  • Meet & greet at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Transfer to Darjeeling via scenic Mirik
  • Visit Mirik - famous for:
  • Man-made Sumendu Lake
  • Cardamom plantations
  • Tea estates
  • Boating opportunity (direct payment basis)
  • Stop at Indo-Nepal border for shopping
  • Continue drive to Darjeeling
  • Check-in to hotel on arrival
  • Evening free for leisure
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing

Early Morning (4:00 AM) - 3 Points:

  • Tiger Hill - Spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • Ghoom Monastery
  • Batasia Loop

After Breakfast (9:30 AM) - 8 Points:

  • Ropeway (time permitting)
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed)
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed)
  • Museum (Thursday closed)
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed)
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden & Processing (Sunday closed)
  • Japanese Temple

Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out from hotel
  • Direct transfer to NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Tour ends
