DPTT08

Himalayan Ecstasy

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Perfect combination tour starting with Darjeeling via scenic Mirik and ending in Gangtok, Sikkim. Ideal for travelers planning to explore both West Bengal and Sikkim hills.

Route: NJP/IXB → Darjeeling via Mirik (2 nights) → Gangtok

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival via Mirik

  • Airport/Station pickup
  • Drive to Darjeeling via Mirik
  • Visit Mirik Lake, tea estates
  • Indo-Nepal border stop
  • Continue to Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Sightseeing

  • Early morning: Tiger Hill sunrise
  • Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop
  • Full day Darjeeling local sightseeing
  • All major 8 points covered

Day 3: Transfer to Gangtok

  • Check out after breakfast
  • Drive to Gangtok via Lamahatta viewpoint
  • Check-in to hotel in Gangtok
  • Tour ends (extend Sikkim tour if needed)
