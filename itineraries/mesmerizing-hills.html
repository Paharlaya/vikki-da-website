DPTT16

Mesmerizing Hills

3 Nights / 4 Days

Overview

Experience the mesmerizing beauty of Darjeeling via the scenic Mirik route, combined with a delightful exploration of Kalimpong. This tour offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and mountain serenity. Enjoy the pristine Sumendu Lake at Mirik, witness the spectacular sunrise at Tiger Hill, and explore the flower nurseries and monasteries of Kalimpong.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling via Mirik → Kalimpong Excursion → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival via Scenic Mirik

  • Meet & greet at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Transfer to Darjeeling (6,710 ft) via Mirik
  • Visit Mirik's attractions:
  • - Man-made Sumendu Lake
  • - Cardamom plantations
  • - Tea estates
  • - Boating (optional, on direct payment basis)
  • Stop at Indo-Nepal border for shopping
  • Continue drive to Darjeeling
  • Check-in to hotel on arrival
  • Evening free for shopping or leisure
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing

Early Morning (4:00 AM) - 3 Points:

  • Tiger Hill - Watch spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • Ghoom Monastery - Historic Buddhist monastery
  • Batasia Loop - Engineering marvel with war memorial

After Breakfast (9:30 AM) - 8 Points:

  • Ropeway (time permitting)
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed)
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed)
  • Museum (Thursday closed)
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed)
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden & Processing (Sunday closed)
  • Japanese Temple

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Kalimpong Excursion

After breakfast, full day excursion to Kalimpong:

  • Lamahatta - Scenic eco-tourism village
  • Lovers View Point - Panoramic mountain views
  • Durpin Dara - Hill viewpoint with monastery
  • Deolo Hill - Highest point in Kalimpong
  • Science Centre - Interactive exhibits
  • Flower Nurseries - Exotic orchids and cacti
  • Return to Darjeeling by evening
  • Evening free for shopping or leisure
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 4: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out from hotel
  • Transfer to NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Tour ends with mesmerizing memories
