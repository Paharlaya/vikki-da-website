DPTT06

Himalayan Glory

3 Nights / 4 Days

Overview

Experience the heritage of Darjeeling with the world-famous Toy Train ride. This tour combines classic sightseeing with the UNESCO World Heritage Darjeeling Himalayan Railway experience.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling (3 nights with Toy Train) → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

  • Airport/Station pickup
  • Transfer to Darjeeling
  • Hotel check-in
  • Evening free

Day 2: Early Morning Sightseeing + Toy Train

Early Morning:

  • Tiger Hill sunrise
  • Ghoom Monastery
  • Batasia Loop

After Breakfast:

  • Drop to Darjeeling Station for Toy Train ride
  • Experience the UNESCO Heritage Railway
  • Enjoy scenic mountain views from train

Day 3: Full Day Sightseeing

  • Rock Garden waterfalls
  • Ropeway (time permitting)
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute
  • Padmaja Naidu Zoo
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Centre
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden
  • Japanese Temple

Day 4: Departure

  • Check out after breakfast
  • Transfer to airport/station
  • Tour ends

Special Features

  • UNESCO World Heritage Toy Train experience
  • Scenic railway journey through mountains
  • Rock Garden waterfalls visit
  • Complete Darjeeling sightseeing
