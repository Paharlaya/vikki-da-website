DPTT13

Highland Sojourn

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Journey from Darjeeling to the mystical town of Pelling in West Sikkim. Experience Darjeeling's charm via Mirik and end at Pelling with spectacular views of Khangchendzongha range.

Route: NJP/IXB → Darjeeling via Mirik (2 nights) → Pelling

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival via Mirik

  • Airport/Station pickup
  • Drive to Darjeeling via scenic Mirik
  • Visit Mirik Lake and surroundings
  • Tea estates and cardamom plantations
  • Check-in at Darjeeling hotel

Day 2: Darjeeling Full Sightseeing

  • 4:00 AM - Tiger Hill sunrise
  • Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop
  • Complete 8-point local sightseeing
  • Ropeway, HMI, Zoo, Tea Garden
  • Japanese Temple visit

Day 3: Transfer to Pelling

  • Check out after breakfast
  • Drive to Pelling, West Sikkim
  • Scenic mountain roads
  • Check-in at Pelling hotel
  • Tour ends (extend Sikkim tour if needed)

Why Choose This Tour?

  • Perfect gateway to West Sikkim
  • Scenic Mirik experience included
  • Complete Darjeeling coverage
  • Ends at peaceful Pelling
