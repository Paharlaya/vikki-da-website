DPTT07

Green Impression

5 Nights / 6 Days

Overview

The ultimate North Bengal hills experience combining everything - Darjeeling's colonial charm, Toy Train heritage ride, Mirik's tranquil lake, and Kalimpong's flower markets. This comprehensive tour covers all major attractions at a comfortable pace.

Starting Point: IXB/NJP/Siliguri to Darjeeling
Ending Point: Darjeeling to IXB/NJP/Siliguri

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport – Darjeeling

  • Meet & greet at airport/station & transfer to Darjeeling
  • Drive to Darjeeling.
  • On arrival check-in to hotel

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing (07 Points)

After Breakfast (09:30 AM) – 08 Points

  • Ropeway
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed,
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed),
  • Museum (Thursday closed),
  • Tenzing Rock,
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed),
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden & Tea Processing (Sunday closed),
  • Japanese Temple,

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 3: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing (03 +01 Points & TOY TRAIN JOY RIDE)

Early Morning (at 04:00 AM) – 04 Points

  • Tiger Hill to watch the spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • On your way back visit Ghoom Monastery and
  • Batasia Loop.
  • Rock Garden waterfalls

After Breakfast (09:30 AM)

Drop to station for TOY TRAIN RIDE (CAB WILL DROP THE GUEST TO DARJEELING STATION AND THE CAB SHALL LEAVE. PICK UP FROM THE STATION CAN BE ARRANGED AT THE STATION BY THE GUEST ON DIRECT PAYMENT BASIS)

Day 4: Darjeeling – Mirik Excursion

After breakfast

  • Drive for an excursion to Mirik and Indo – Nepal border.
  • Stop at Indo Nepal border for shopping (transport into Nepal on direct payment basis in Nepal)
  • Mirik is famous for its
  • Man-made Sumendu Lake,
  • Cardamom plantations
  • Tea estates &
  • Boating (one can do boating on direct payment basis at boating counter)
  • After Mirik visit, drive back to Darjeeling.

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 5: Darjeeling – Kalimpong

After breakfast drive to Kalimpong

  • Lamahatta
  • View Point
  • Mangal Dham
  • Durpin Dara
  • Golf Course
  • Deolo Hill
  • Science Centre
  • Flower Nurseries

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 6: Darjeeling – NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport

  • After breakfast check out from hotel on time and take the departure transfer to NJP railway station / IXB airport for your onward journey.
  • Tour ends
