DPTT07

Green Impression

5 Nights / 6 Days

Overview

The ultimate North Bengal hills experience combining everything - Darjeeling's colonial charm, Toy Train heritage ride, Mirik's tranquil lake, and Kalimpong's flower markets. This comprehensive tour covers all major attractions at a comfortable pace.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

  • Airport/Station pickup and transfer to Darjeeling
  • Hotel check-in and rest

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing (7 Points)

  • Ropeway, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute
  • Padmaja Naidu Zoo, Museum
  • Tenzing Rock, Tibetan Refugee Centre
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden, Japanese Temple

Day 3: Early Morning Sightseeing + Toy Train

  • 4:00 AM - Tiger Hill, Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop
  • Rock Garden waterfalls
  • Afternoon: UNESCO Toy Train joy ride

Day 4: Mirik Excursion

  • Full day excursion to Mirik
  • Sumendu Lake, tea estates, cardamom plantations
  • Indo-Nepal border shopping
  • Return to Darjeeling

Day 5: Kalimpong Excursion

  • Drive to Kalimpong via Lamahatta viewpoint
  • Visit Durpin Dara, Golf Course, Deolo Hill
  • Science Centre, flower nurseries
  • Return to Darjeeling

Day 6: Departure

  • Check out and transfer to airport/station
