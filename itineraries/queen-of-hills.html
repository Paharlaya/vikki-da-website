DPTT01

Queen of Hills

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Experience the timeless charm of Darjeeling, the "Queen of Hills". This classic tour covers all major attractions including the spectacular sunrise at Tiger Hill, the historic Ghoom Monastery, and the famous Batasia Loop. Perfect for first-time visitors seeking to explore Darjeeling's natural beauty and colonial heritage.

Starting Point: IXB Airport/NJP Station/Siliguri to Darjeeling
Ending Point: Darjeeling to IXB Airport/NJP Station/Siliguri

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport – Darjeeling

  • Meet & greet at airport/station & transfer to Darjeeling
  • Drive to Darjeeling. On arrival check-in to hotel.
  • Evening free for shopping or leisure.
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing (03 Points & 07 Points)

Early Morning (at 04:00 AM) – 03 Points

  • Tiger Hill to watch the spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • On your way back visit Ghoom Monastery and
  • Batasia Loop.

After Breakfast (09:30 AM) – 08 Points

  • Ropeway (TIME PERMITTING)
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed,
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed),
  • Museum (Thursday closed),
  • Tenzing Rock,
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed),
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden & Tea Processing (Sunday closed),
  • Japanese Temple,

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 3: Darjeeling to NJP Station/IXB Airport

  • After breakfast check out from hotel on time and take the departure transfer to NJP railway station / IXB airport for your onward journey.
  • Tour ends
