DPTT01

Queen of Hills

2 Nights / 3 Days

Overview

Experience the timeless charm of Darjeeling, the "Queen of Hills". This classic tour covers all major attractions including the spectacular sunrise at Tiger Hill, the historic Ghoom Monastery, and the famous Batasia Loop. Perfect for first-time visitors seeking to explore Darjeeling's natural beauty and colonial heritage.

Route: NJP/IXB Airport → Darjeeling (2 nights) → NJP/IXB Airport

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling

  • Meet & greet at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Transfer to Darjeeling (6,710 ft)
  • Drive through scenic mountain roads
  • Check-in to hotel on arrival
  • Evening free for shopping or leisure
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling

Day 2: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing

Early Morning (4:00 AM) - 3 Points:

  • Tiger Hill - Watch spectacular sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzongha
  • Ghoom Monastery - Historic Buddhist monastery
  • Batasia Loop - Engineering marvel with war memorial

After Breakfast (9:30 AM) - 7 Points:

  • Ropeway (time permitting)
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Thursday closed)
  • P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed)
  • Museum (Thursday closed)
  • Tenzing Rock
  • Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (Sunday closed)
  • Happy Valley Tea Garden & Processing (Sunday closed)
  • Japanese Temple

Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out from hotel
  • Transfer to NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport
  • Tour ends with sweet memories
